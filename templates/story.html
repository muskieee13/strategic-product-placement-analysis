Key Takeaways

Electronics leads in revenue — despite fewer transactions, high price points drive the most revenue.

Clothing dominates transaction volume — the most purchased category across all demographics.

Seasonal trends matter — sales peaks align with holiday and festive seasons.

Age 26–45 drives spending — this demographic contributes the highest share of total revenue.

Gender-neutral strategies work best — male and female spending patterns are relatively balanced.

Strategic pricing boosts revenue — premium-priced items in the right category maximize returns.
